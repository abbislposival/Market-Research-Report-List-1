<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Proportional Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Valves with Bender Element</li><li>Jet Valves with Multilayer Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Piezoelectric Proportional Valve market consists of two primary types: valves with bender elements and jet valves featuring multilayer actuators. Valves with bender elements leverage the bending motion of piezoelectric materials to regulate fluid flow precisely, offering rapid response times and high accuracy. In contrast, jet valves with multilayer actuators utilize stacked piezoelectric layers to achieve efficient and compact actuation, allowing for precise control, reduced energy consumption, and enhanced reliability in various applications, including industrial automation and fluid control systems.</p></p>
