<p><p>Grain and seed cleaning equipment is essential for ensuring high quality and purity in agricultural products. The market is divided into two main types: pre-cleaning and fine cleaning. Pre-cleaning equipment is designed to remove larger impurities such as straw, dirt, and rocks from the grain or seed, preparing it for further processing. Fine cleaning equipment, on the other hand, focuses on eliminating smaller contaminants, including dust, chaff, and other minute particles, enhancing the overall quality of the final product.</p></p>
