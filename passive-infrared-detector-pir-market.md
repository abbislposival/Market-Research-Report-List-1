<p><p>Passive Infrared Detectors (PIR) detect infrared radiation emitted by objects, primarily for motion detection in security and automation systems. The market can be categorized into single-element and dual-element detectors. Single-element PIRs provide basic detection over a limited range, while dual-element PIRs offer enhanced sensitivity and coverage through two sensing elements for more accurate motion detection. Other types may include multi-element and microwave-integrated detectors, which combine various technologies to improve performance, reliability, and response in diverse applications.</p></p>
