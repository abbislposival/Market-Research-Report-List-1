<p><strong>The Rigid Knee Braces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prophylactic Braces</li><li>Functional Braces</li><li>Rehabilitative Braces</li><li>Unloader Braces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid knee braces market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Prophylactic Braces**: Designed to prevent injuries during sports activities by providing stability and support.</p><p>2. **Functional Braces**: Used post-injury to aid recovery, offering support and enhancing mobility during rehabilitation.</p><p>3. **Rehabilitative Braces**: Focused on recovery, these braces limit movement to facilitate healing while ensuring protection.</p><p>4. **Unloader Braces**: Specifically for osteoarthritis, these braces redistribute weight away from the affected knee compartment, alleviating pain and improving function.</p></p>
