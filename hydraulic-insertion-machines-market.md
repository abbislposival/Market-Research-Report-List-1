<p><p>Hydraulic insertion machines are categorized into three market types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operator intervention for each insertion, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process by automating certain steps while still needing human input, enhancing efficiency without full automation. Fully automatic machines perform all insertion tasks independently, maximizing speed and reducing labor costs, making them ideal for high-volume production environments. Each type serves different operational needs and budget considerations in manufacturing.</p></p>
