<p><p>The Estradiol Test Kit market includes various types, primarily categorized by their detection methods: Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRF utilizes fluorescent labels and time-resolved detection to ensure high sensitivity and specificity in measuring estradiol levels. In contrast, CLIA employs luminescent signals generated by chemiluminescent substrates, providing rapid and precise results. Both methods are crucial for evaluating hormone levels in clinical settings, with applications in reproductive health, endocrine assessments, and related research.</p></p>
