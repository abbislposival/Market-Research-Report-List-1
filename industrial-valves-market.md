<p><p>The industrial valves market comprises several types, each serving distinct functions. Ball valves allow or block flow using a spherical closure element. Globe valves regulate flow with a movable disk. Gate valves control flow by raising or lowering a gate-like element. Butterfly valves manage flow with a rotating disc. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring fluid moves in one direction. Other valve types may include plug valves, needle valves, and safety valves, catering to specific applications across various industries.</p></p>
