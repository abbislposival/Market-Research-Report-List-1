<p><p>Fiberglass Reinforced Plastics (FRP) are composite materials made by combining fiberglass with a polymer matrix, enhancing strength and durability. The market for FRP is divided into two main types: thermosetting plastic products and thermoplastic plastic products. </p><p>Thermosetting plastics harden permanently upon curing, providing excellent chemical resistance and structural integrity, suitable for applications like automotive or construction. Conversely, thermoplastic products can be reshaped upon heating, offering flexibility and recyclability, making them ideal for industries like consumer goods and aerospace.</p></p>
