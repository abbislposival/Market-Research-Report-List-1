<p><p>The Automotive Intelligence Battery market encompasses various communication protocols used for vehicle battery management systems. LIN (Local Interconnect Network) is a simple, low-cost network for sensor data transfer and diagnostics. MCU (Microcontroller Unit) serves as the processing hub for battery management, handling data and ensuring optimal performance. CAN (Controller Area Network) facilitates higher-speed communication, allowing multiple controllers to stay synchronized for complex automotive applications. Together, these types enhance battery monitoring, efficiency, and overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
