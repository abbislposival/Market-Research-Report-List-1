<p><ul><li>Hot Spark Plugs</li><li>Cold Spark Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sparkplug market is divided into two main types: hot and cold spark plugs. Hot spark plugs operate at higher temperatures, preventing fouling and ensuring smoother engine performance in low-compression engines. They are ideal for everyday driving conditions. In contrast, cold spark plugs are designed for high-performance engines, dissipating heat quickly to prevent pre-ignition. They are essential for racing or high-speed applications where intense heat build-up occurs. Selecting the right type enhances engine efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
