<p><p>Elastomer bearings are critical components in infrastructure, offering flexibility and support for structures like bridges and buildings. The market features two main types: plain bearings, which consist of a single layer of elastomer and provide simple cushioning and movement absorption; and laminated bearings, which comprise multiple layers of elastomer and steel, offering enhanced load capacity and resilience. Each type serves specific applications, with plain bearings favored for straightforward tasks and laminated bearings preferred for complex load demands and seismic resistance.</p></p>
