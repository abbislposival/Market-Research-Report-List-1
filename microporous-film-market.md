<p><p>The microporous film market is primarily categorized into unidirectional stretching and biaxial stretching types. Unidirectional stretching involves the elongation of the film in one direction, enhancing its tensile strength and permeability characteristics. This type is often utilized in applications requiring specific airflow properties. Biaxial stretching, on the other hand, entails stretching the film in two perpendicular directions, which improves overall toughness, barrier properties, and clarity. Both types cater to diverse sectors including packaging, medical, and automotive industries, depending on performance needs.</p></p>
