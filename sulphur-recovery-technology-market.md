<p><p>The Sulphur Recovery Technology market primarily consists of three key types: the Claus Process, which is the standard method for converting hydrogen sulfide into elemental sulfur; the Modified Claus Process, an enhanced version that increases conversion efficiency; and Tail Gas Treatment, which involves additional processes to handle leftover gases from the Claus process, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. Collectively, these technologies are essential for minimizing sulfur emissions and recovering valuable sulfur from industrial processes, particularly in oil and gas production.</p></p>
