<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pectin market is categorized into three main types based on methoxyl content. High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, typically derived from citrus fruits, requires sugar and acid to gel, making it ideal for jams and jellies. Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin, on the other hand, can gel with less sugar, suitable for low-sugar products. Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin, modified chemically, offers greater flexibility in gelling conditions and is employed in a variety of food applications including dairy and confectionery products.</p></p>
