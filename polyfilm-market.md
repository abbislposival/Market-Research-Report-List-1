<p><p>The Polyfilm market includes various types of films used in packaging, agriculture, and industrial applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and moisture resistance. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) provides higher tensile strength and puncture resistance. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is rigid and durable. BoPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) is known for clarity and stiffness. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) is heat-sealable and transparent. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile and cost-effective. BoPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) offers high strength and barrier properties. BoPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) provides excellent gas barrier and toughness. Others include specialty films for niche applications.</p></p>
