<p><strong>The Electro Hydraulic Servo Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nozzle Flapper Valve</li><li>Jet Action Valve</li><li>Direct Drive Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-hydraulic servo valve market includes various types, such as nozzle flapper valves, jet action valves, and direct drive valves. Nozzle flapper valves use a flapper mechanism to control fluid flow by varying nozzle openings, providing high precision. Jet action valves utilize high-velocity jets to achieve rapid response and accuracy in controlling hydraulic systems. Direct drive valves feature electromagnetic actuators for direct control of fluid flow, allowing for faster response times and enhanced performance in automated applications.</p></p>
