<p><p>The in-vitro toxicology testing market encompasses various methodologies. Cellular assays involve testing substances on living cells to assess toxicity and biological responses. Biochemical assays analyze the effects of chemicals on biological pathways, often using enzymes or proteins. In silico methods utilize computer models and simulations to predict toxic effects based on chemical structure. Ex-vivo testing examines biological samples from organisms outside their normal biological context, providing insights into toxicological effects while minimizing the use of live animals.</p></p>
