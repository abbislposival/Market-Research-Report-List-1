<p><p>The online course software market can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access courses from anywhere with an internet connection, offering scalability, automatic updates, and reduced IT maintenance. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on organizations' servers, providing greater control over data and customization options, but requiring significant initial investment and ongoing maintenance.
