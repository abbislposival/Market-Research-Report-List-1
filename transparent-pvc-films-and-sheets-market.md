<p><p>Transparent PVC films and sheets are produced through various technologies, each catering to specific applications. Calendering technology involves passing resin through heated rollers to create thin, smooth sheets ideal for packaging and printing. Extrusion technology melts PVC and forms continuous sheets or films, offering versatility for various protective applications. Laminating technology combines multiple layers, enhancing durability and aesthetics, often used in signage and protective covers. Each method contributes uniquely to the transparent PVC market, meeting diverse industry needs.</p></p>
