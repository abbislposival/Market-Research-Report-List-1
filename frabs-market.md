<p><p>FRABS market types include non-halogen and halogen types, differentiated by the materials used for flame retardancy. Non-halogen FRABS utilize alternative compounds that offer safety without the environmental concerns associated with halogens, appealing to eco-conscious industries. In contrast, halogen-type FRABS contain halogenated compounds, providing effective flame retardancy but often raising environmental and health concerns. Both types cater to diverse applications, from construction materials to electronics, balancing performance requirements and regulatory standards in fire safety.</p></p>
