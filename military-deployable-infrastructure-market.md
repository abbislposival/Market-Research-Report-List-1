<p><p>The Military Deployable Infrastructure market is divided into two main types: soft deployable and hard deployable infrastructure. Soft deployable infrastructure encompasses lightweight, flexible systems such as tentage, portable shelters, and rapidly deployable medical facilities, designed for quick setup and mobility. In contrast, hard deployable infrastructure refers to robust, more permanent installations like pre-fabricated buildings, command centers, and storage facilities, which offer enhanced durability and security but require more time and resources to deploy. Both types support operational readiness in various military scenarios.</p></p>
