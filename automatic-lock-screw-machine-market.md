<p><p>The Automatic Lock Screw Machine market is categorized into two primary types: Active Feed and Manual Feed. Active Feed machines utilize automated systems to continuously supply screws, enhancing efficiency and precision in assembly processes. In contrast, Manual Feed machines require operators to feed screws individually, offering greater control but potentially slowing production. Both types serve distinct applications, with Active Feed being favored in high-volume settings, while Manual Feed is suitable for smaller operations or specialized tasks requiring manual intervention.</p></p>
