<p><p>The building waterproof material market comprises several types designed to prevent water infiltration. Cementitious waterproofing uses a cement-based mixture, ideal for surfaces like basements. Liquid waterproofing membranes offer flexible, seamless protection, suitable for various applications. Bituminous membranes are asphalt-based, providing robust barriers for roofs and foundations. Bituminous coatings are applied as a liquid to form a protective layer on surfaces. Polyurethane liquid membranes are versatile and durable, often used for rooftops and terraces due to their elasticity and resistance to weathering.</p></p>
