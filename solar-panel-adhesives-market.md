<p><p>The solar panel adhesives market comprises two primary types: epoxy and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives offer strong bonding strength and excellent durability, making them ideal for high-stress applications and environments with extreme temperatures. They exhibit superior resistance to chemicals and moisture. In contrast, polyurethane adhesives are more flexible, providing better cushioning and shock absorption, which can accommodate thermal expansion and contraction. This flexibility makes them suitable for different substrate materials, enhancing the overall durability and longevity of solar panel installations.</p></p>
