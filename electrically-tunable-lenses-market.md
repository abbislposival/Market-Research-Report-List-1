<p><p>The electrically tunable lenses market features two primary types: flat convex and flat concave lenses. Flat convex lenses are designed to focus light more effectively and are often used in applications requiring zoom capabilities, such as cameras and projectors. In contrast, flat concave lenses diverge light, making them suitable for applications like beam shaping and optical corrections. Both types utilize adjustable optical properties, driven by electric signals, to enhance image quality and flexibility in various optical systems.</p></p>
