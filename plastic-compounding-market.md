<p><p>The plastic compounding market consists of several types based on the polymer used. PP (Polypropylene) compounds offer excellent chemical resistance and durability, primarily used in automotive and packaging applications. PE (Polyethylene) compounds are known for their flexibility and moisture resistance, ideal for film and container manufacturing. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) compounds exhibit good electrical insulation properties, commonly used in construction and plumbing. PA (Polyamide) compounds, known for their strength and heat resistance, are used in textiles and automotive components. The “Others” category includes various engineered plastics and specialty compounds.</p></p>
