<p><p>The alarm buzzer market is primarily divided into two types: active buzzers and passive buzzers. Active buzzers generate sound independently when powered, using internal circuitry, and are typically easier to integrate into systems. In contrast, passive buzzers require an external signal or frequency to produce sound, making them more versatile in applications requiring specific tones. Together, these products serve various sectors, including consumer electronics, security systems, and industrial applications, catering to diverse sound signaling needs.</p></p>
