The 2 plate machines utilize two folding plates to create basic folds, making them suitable for simpler tasks and lower production volumes. In contrast, 4 plate paper folding machines incorporate four plates, allowing for more complex folds and higher efficiency, catering to larger production needs. Both types are essential for businesses requiring precise and efficient paper folding solutions.</p></p>
