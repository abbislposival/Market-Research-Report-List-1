Manual microtomes require direct operator control for sample sectioning, offering flexibility but demanding skill and time. Semi-automatic devices enhance efficiency by integrating some automated functions, reducing operator effort while maintaining control over the process. Automatic cryostat microtomes provide full automation, maximizing throughput and consistency in sample preparation, thereby catering to high-demand laboratory environments and enhancing workflow efficiency in histological applications.</p></p>
