<p><p>Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs) are materials made by combining a polymer resin (matrix) with reinforcing fibers, enhancing strength and durability. The PMC market is primarily categorized into two types: thermosets and thermoplastics. Thermosetting composites, once cured, cannot be re-molded, offering excellent thermal stability and rigidity, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Thermoplastic composites, on the other hand, can be reprocessed and reshaped, providing versatility and ease of manufacturing, suitable for consumer goods and industrial uses.</p></p>
