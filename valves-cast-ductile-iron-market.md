<p><p>The cast and ductile iron valve market encompasses various types, each serving unique functions. Butterfly valves efficiently regulate flow with a rotating disc. Gate valves provide a straight flow path, ideal for on/off control. Globe valves offer precise flow regulation through a globe-shaped body, while ball valves ensure quick shutoff with a spherical closure. Check valves prevent backflow in piping systems, maintaining system integrity. These valves are essential in industries such as water treatment, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p></p>
