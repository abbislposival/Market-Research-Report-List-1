<p><p>The self-adhesive labels market includes various printing types tailored to specific needs. **Offset Print** offers high-quality, cost-effective labels for large runs. **Flexography Print** is ideal for flexible packaging, using fast-drying inks on diverse materials. **Rotogravure Print** delivers high-resolution, durable labels for long print runs. **Screen Print** provides vibrant colors and thick ink layers for specialty labels. **Letterpress Print** is used for simple, tactile designs on shorter runs. **Digital Print** enables customization, variable data, and quick turnaround for small to medium batches. Each method suits different applications and production scales.</p></p>
