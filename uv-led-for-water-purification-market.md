<p><strong>The UV LED for Water Purification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV LED water purification market consists of three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs emit wavelengths between 320-400 nm, primarily used for disinfection and algae control. UV-B LEDs operate in the 280-320 nm range, effective for germicidal applications but less common in water treatments. UV-C LEDs, emitting wavelengths between 200-280 nm, are the most widely employed in water purification due to their efficiency in inactivating bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water.</p></p>
