<p><p>The truck suspension system market comprises several types that enhance vehicle performance and comfort. Air suspension uses air-filled bags to absorb shocks, providing a smooth ride and adjustable load capacity. Hydraulic suspension employs fluid movement to dampen impacts, offering stability and improved control. Electromagnetic suspension utilizes magnetic fields to adjust stiffness dynamically for superior handling, while electro-hydraulic suspension combines hydraulic systems with electronic controls for precision in ride quality and responsiveness. These systems cater to varying load conditions and driving environments.</p></p>
