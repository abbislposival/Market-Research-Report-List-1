<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) communication encompasses various components vital for effective data transmission. Transmitters convert electrical signals into light signals, while receivers capture and convert light back into electrical signals. Modulators encode data onto light waves for transmission, and demodulators extract this data at the receiving end. Encoders prepare information for modulation, ensuring it adheres to specific formats, whereas decoders interpret the received signals into usable information. Together, these components facilitate high-speed, wireless communication using optical technologies.</p></p>
