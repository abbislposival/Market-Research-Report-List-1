<p><strong>The Artificial Heart Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare-metal Stent (BMS)</li><li>Drug-eluting Stent (DES)</li><li>Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffold (BVS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial heart stent market comprises three main types: Bare-metal Stents (BMS), which are simple metal devices that provide structural support; Drug-eluting Stents (DES), which are coated with medication to prevent restenosis by inhibiting cell growth; and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds (BVS), designed to gradually dissolve and encourage natural vessel healing over time. Each type caters to specific patient needs, balancing ease of use, risk of complications, and long-term effectiveness in treating coronary artery diseases.</p></p>
