<p><p>The automatic pulp moulding machines market is primarily categorized into two types: rotary pulp moulding machines and reciprocating pulp moulding machines. Rotary pulp moulding machines operate on a continuous cycle, providing high production efficiency and are ideal for producing large volumes of molded products. In contrast, reciprocating pulp moulding machines employ a back-and-forth motion, allowing for greater flexibility in product design and the ability to handle varied production requirements. Both types serve critical roles in manufacturing eco-friendly packaging solutions.</p></p>
