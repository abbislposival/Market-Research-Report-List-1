<p><p>X-ray Diffraction (XRD) equipment encompasses two primary market types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD is utilized for analyzing polycrystalline samples, providing detailed crystallographic information and phase identification. It is widely used in materials science and chemistry. In contrast, Single-crystal XRD targets individual crystal samples, offering precise structural data about the atomic arrangement, which is essential in fields like chemistry and solid-state physics. Both techniques serve critical roles in material characterization and development.</p></p>
