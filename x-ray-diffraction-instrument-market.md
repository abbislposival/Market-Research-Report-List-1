<p><ul><li>Powder XRD</li><li>Single-crystal XRD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray Diffraction (XRD) instrument market comprises two main types: Powder XRD and Single-Crystal XRD. Powder XRD analyzes crystalline materials in powdered form, providing average structural information, which is essential for identifying phases and determining crystallite size. In contrast, Single-Crystal XRD evaluates individual crystal samples to yield highly precise structural data, revealing atomic arrangements and interatomic distances. Both techniques play a vital role in material characterization across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, materials science, and geology.</p></p>
