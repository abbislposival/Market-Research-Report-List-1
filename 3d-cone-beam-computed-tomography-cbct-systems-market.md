<p><strong>The 3D Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier Systems</li><li>Flat-Panel Imager Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) systems market is characterized by two primary types: Image Intensifier Systems and Flat-Panel Imager Systems. Image Intensifier Systems utilize a traditional radiographic approach, converting X-ray photons into visible light, which is then captured to generate images. Conversely, Flat-Panel Imager Systems employ digital detectors to directly capture X-ray images with improved resolution and lower radiation doses. These systems are widely used in dental, orthopedic, and other medical imaging applications due to their accurate 3D reconstruction capabilities.</p></p>
