<p><p>The nasal spray flu vaccine market primarily consists of two types: trivalent and quadrivalent flu vaccines. In contrast, quadrivalent flu vaccines include an additional B strain, offering broader protection. Both vaccine types are delivered via a nasal spray, making them needle-free alternatives aimed at improving vaccination uptake, especially among children and those averse to injections.</p></p>
