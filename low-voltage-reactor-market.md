<p><p>The Low Voltage Reactor market encompasses two primary types: dry-type reactors and oil-immersed reactors. Dry-type reactors utilize air for cooling, making them safer and maintenance-friendly, ideal for indoor applications. Conversely, oil-immersed reactors are cooled by insulating oil, allowing for higher power handling and efficiency in outdoor settings. Both types serve to manage voltage levels and improve the stability of electrical systems, catering to diverse industrial, commercial, and utility applications while addressing safety and operational needs.</p></p>
