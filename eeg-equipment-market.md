<p><strong>The EEG Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional EEG</li><li>Video EEG</li><li>Dynamic EEG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EEG equipment market encompasses various types, including Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG captures electrical brain activity through electrodes, primarily for diagnosing conditions like epilepsy. Video EEG combines brain activity monitoring with simultaneous video recording to correlate seizures with behavior. Dynamic EEG, on the other hand, involves continuous monitoring over extended periods, often in a patient’s natural environment, providing comprehensive data for more accurate diagnoses. Each type serves distinct clinical needs and enhances patient care.</p></p>
