<p><p>The Burnt Dolomite market comprises two primary types: Dead Burnt Dolomite and Light Burnt Dolomite. Dead Burnt Dolomite is produced by calcining dolomite at high temperatures, resulting in a dense, refractory material used in steelmaking, cement, and glass industries. Light Burnt Dolomite, calcined at lower temperatures, retains some reactivity and is utilized in agriculture, water treatment, and construction.
