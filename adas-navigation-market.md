<p><p>ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) Navigation Market comprises various sensor types, each playing a crucial role in vehicle automation. Image sensors capture visual data for lane detection and object recognition. RADAR sensors detect distance and speed of objects, enhancing collision avoidance. Laser sensors, or LiDAR, provide detailed 3D mapping for precise navigation. Ultrasonic sensors detect proximity for parking assistance, while infrared sensors enhance visibility in low-light conditions. Other sensors may include GPS and inertial measurement units, contributing to comprehensive vehicular safety and navigation.</p></p>
