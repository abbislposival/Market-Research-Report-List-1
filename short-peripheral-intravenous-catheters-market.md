<p><p>The Short Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) market is categorized into two main types: ported and non-ported PIVCs. Ported short PIVCs feature a built-in port that allows for easy medication administration and blood sample collection without needing to replace the catheter, enhancing convenience and reducing patient trauma. In contrast, non-ported short PIVCs lack this additional access point, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for straightforward intravenous therapy. Both types cater to diverse clinical needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
