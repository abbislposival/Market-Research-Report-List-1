<p><p>The metal chrome market is typically segmented into two main types: lump and powder. Lump chrome refers to the natural ore form, often used in applications like metallurgy and ferrochrome production, where high purity and larger quantities are required. Powder chrome, on the other hand, is processed into finer particles, catering to uses in coatings, ceramics, and chemical industries. Both types serve distinct applications, with the choice between them depending on the specific requirements of the end-use industry.</p></p>
