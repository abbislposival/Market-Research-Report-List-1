<p><p>The transmission pump market is primarily divided into two types: fixed displacement pumps and variable displacement pumps. Fixed displacement pumps provide a constant flow of fluid at a set rate, ensuring consistent performance but lacking flexibility in flow adjustments. In contrast, variable displacement pumps can adjust their output based on demand, improving efficiency and reducing energy consumption in various applications. Both types are essential in automotive and industrial sectors, serving different needs based on performance requirements and operational efficiency.</p></p>
