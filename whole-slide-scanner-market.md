<p><strong>The Whole Slide Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brightfield</li><li>Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The whole slide scanner market encompasses two primary types: brightfield and fluorescence. Brightfield scanners are designed to capture high-resolution images of tissue samples stained with traditional dyes, providing clear visibility of structural details. In contrast, fluorescence scanners utilize specific wavelengths of light to excite fluorescently labeled samples, enabling visualization of cellular processes and biomarker expression. Both types serve critical roles in pathology, research, and diagnostics, facilitating accurate analysis and enhancing the understanding of biological specimens.</p></p>
