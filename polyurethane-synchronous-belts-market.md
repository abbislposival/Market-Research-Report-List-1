<p><p>The polyurethane synchronous belts market comprises two primary types: thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) and thermoset polyurethane (TSR). Thermoplastic PU belts are flexible, allowing for easy processing and recycling, making them suitable for various applications requiring durability and resistance to wear. In contrast, thermoset PU belts are chemically cross-linked for enhanced strength, heat resistance, and dimensional stability, ideal for extreme conditions. Both types cater to different industrial requirements, emphasizing their roles in mechanical power transmission and timing applications.</p></p>
