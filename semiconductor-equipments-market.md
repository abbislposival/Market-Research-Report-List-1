<p><p>The semiconductor equipment market comprises various specialized segments essential for semiconductor fabrication. Etching equipment precisely removes material to create patterns on wafers, while thin film deposition equipment deposits layers of materials to form semiconductor devices. Lithography equipment projects intricate designs onto wafers, facilitating circuit formation. Semiconductor process control equipment monitors and optimizes manufacturing processes for quality assurance. Finally, compound semiconductor equipment focuses on producing materials like gallium nitride and indium phosphide, critical for advanced electronic and optoelectronic applications.</p></p>
