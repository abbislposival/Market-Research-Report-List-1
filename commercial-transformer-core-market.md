<p><strong>The Commercial Transformer Core Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Laminated Cores</li><li>Solid Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial transformer core market includes two main types: steel laminated cores and solid cores. Steel laminated cores consist of thin sheets of steel layered together, reducing eddy current losses and improving efficiency. They are commonly used in transformers due to their superior electromagnetic properties. In contrast, solid cores are made from a single piece of magnetic material, offering a simpler construction but generally higher losses. Each type serves different applications, balancing factors like cost, efficiency, and performance demands.</p></p>
