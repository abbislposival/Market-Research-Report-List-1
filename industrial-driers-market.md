<p><p>The industrial dryers market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Rotary dryers facilitate continuous processes by tumbling materials in a heated drum, ideal for bulk solids. Dispersion dryers combine airflow with high temperatures to rapidly remove moisture from pastes and slurries. Fluid bed dryers utilize hot air to create a fluidized state, ensuring uniform drying of granules or powders. Belt dryers employ a continuous conveyor system for gentle drying of products like fruits or vegetables. Other types include vacuum and microwave dryers, serving niche needs.</p></p>
