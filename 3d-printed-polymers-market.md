<p><p>The 3D printed polymers market features prominent technologies like Stereolithography (SLA) and Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM). SLA uses a UV light source to cure liquid resin into solid layers, ideal for high-detail prototypes and intricate designs. Conversely, FDM extrudes thermoplastic filaments layer-by-layer to create parts, making it widely accessible and suitable for functional prototypes. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including automotive, healthcare, and consumer goods, driving innovation in rapid prototyping and customized manufacturing solutions.</p></p>
