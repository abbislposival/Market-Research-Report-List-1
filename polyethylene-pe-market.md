<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) is a widely used plastic with three primary types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE is known for its strength and rigidity, making it ideal for containers and pipes. LDPE is more flexible and used for plastic bags and films. LLDPE, a copolymer, combines attributes of both, offering increased tensile strength and stretchability, often used in flexible packaging and agricultural films, catering to diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
