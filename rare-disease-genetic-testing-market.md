<p><p>The rare disease genetic testing market includes several advanced technologies. Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) provides comprehensive genetic analysis, allowing for rapid sequencing of multiple genes. Array technology offers high-resolution analysis of genetic variations. PCR-based testing amplifies DNA for targeted gene identification. Fluorescence in Situ Hybridization (FISH) visualizes specific genetic material in cells. Sanger sequencing, a traditional method, determines the precise order of nucleotides. Karyotyping analyzes chromosomal structures. Other methods may include techniques like CRISPR and whole genome sequencing, broadening diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
