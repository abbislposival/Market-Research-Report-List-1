<p><p>The vinyl surface coatings market is categorized into three primary types: solvent-based, waterborne (latex), and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings offer excellent durability and adhesion, making them suitable for demanding environments. Waterborne (latex) coatings are environmentally friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and easy to clean, providing a safer option for indoor applications. Powder coatings, applied in a dry powder form, provide a thick, durable finish and are ideal for outdoor use due to their resistance to fading and chipping.</p></p>
