<p><strong>The Automobile Airbag Inflators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic</li><li>Stored Gas</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile airbag inflators market comprises three main types: Pyrotechnic, Stored Gas, and Hybrid. Pyrotechnic inflators use chemical reactions to generate gas rapidly, ensuring quick deployment. Stored Gas inflators rely on pre-pressurized gas, offering smoother deployment with less heat. Hybrid inflators combine both methods, using a small pyrotechnic charge to release stored gas, balancing speed and efficiency. Each type caters to specific safety and performance needs, influencing their adoption in modern vehicles based on cost, reliability, and deployment characteristics.</p></p>
