<p><p>The antimicrobial additives market is categorized into two main types: inorganic and organic antimicrobial additives. Inorganic antimicrobial additives, such as silver and copper compounds, provide antimicrobial properties through their metal ions, often used in plastics, coatings, and textiles for enhanced durability and hygiene. Organic antimicrobial additives, including biocides and natural extracts, are derived from carbon-based compounds, offering flexibility in applications ranging from personal care products to paints. Both types effectively inhibit microbial growth, enhancing product lifespan and safety.</p></p>
