<p><p>The aviation sealant market is divided into two primary types: organic and inorganic sealants. Organic sealants, often based on elastomers, provide flexibility and excellent adhesion, making them suitable for various aircraft applications. In contrast, inorganic sealants, usually composed of silicone or polysiloxane materials, offer superior resistance to extreme temperatures and harsh environmental conditions. Both types are crucial in ensuring the integrity and safety of aircraft structures by preventing leaks and enhancing durability across different aerospace applications.</p></p>
