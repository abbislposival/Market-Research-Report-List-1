<p><p>Equipment safety interlock switches are crucial for ensuring the safe operation of machinery by preventing unauthorized access or unsafe conditions. The market includes two primary types: Magnetic-Force Interlocking, which uses magnetism to hold the switch in a locked position until the entry point is secure, and Spring-Force Interlocking, which utilizes mechanical springs to ensure the switch remains engaged unless deliberately disengaged. Both types enhance safety by signaling when equipment is in operation or at risk, reducing the likelihood of accidents.</p></p>
