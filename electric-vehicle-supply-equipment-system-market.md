<p><p>Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) includes systems that deliver electric power to charge electric vehicles. The market is divided into DC Charging and AC Charging. DC Charging, or fast charging, provides high-voltage direct current, enabling rapid charging times, ideal for commercial stations and long-distance travel. AC Charging, on the other hand, utilizes alternating current typically found in residential and commercial outlets, offering slower charging speeds, suitable for overnight or extended parking scenarios. Both types contribute to the EV infrastructure expansion.</p></p>
