<p><p>Carbon Fibre Reinforced Polymers (CFRPs) are categorized based on the type of resin used. Epoxy resin is known for its excellent adhesion, high strength, and durability, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Polyester resin is cost-effective and widely used in construction and marine industries. Nylon offers flexibility and chemical resistance, suitable for various consumer products. Vinyl ester resin combines the benefits of epoxy and polyester, providing corrosion resistance and superior mechanical properties, often used in demanding environments.</p></p>
