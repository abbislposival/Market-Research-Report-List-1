GDI systems deliver fuel directly into the combustion chamber of gasoline engines, enhancing efficiency, power, and emissions control. DDI systems, used in diesel engines, inject fuel at high pressure directly into the cylinder, optimizing combustion for better fuel economy and reduced emissions. Both technologies improve engine performance and meet stringent emission standards, driving their adoption in modern vehicles.</p></p>
