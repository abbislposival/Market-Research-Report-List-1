<p><ul><li>Alcohol-base Type</li><li>Non-alcohol Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The instrument disinfectants market is primarily divided into two types: alcohol-based and non-alcohol-based disinfectants. Alcohol-based disinfectants, typically containing isopropyl or ethyl alcohol, are effective against a wide range of pathogens and evaporate quickly, making them suitable for rapid disinfection. Non-alcohol-based disinfectants, which may contain chlorine compounds, quaternary ammonium compounds, or hydrogen peroxide, provide prolonged disinfection and may be advantageous for surfaces that cannot tolerate alcohol. Both types are essential in healthcare and other settings for maintaining hygiene and preventing infections.</p></p>
