<p><p>The orogastric tube market comprises three main types: flexible, rigid, and semi-rigid tubes. Flexible tubes are designed for comfort and ease of insertion, making them ideal for prolonged use. Rigid tubes offer stability and are often used in emergency situations for quick placement. Semi-rigid tubes combine characteristics of both, providing moderate flexibility for better adaptability while maintaining a certain level of structural integrity. Each type serves distinct clinical needs based on patient condition and procedural requirements.</p></p>
