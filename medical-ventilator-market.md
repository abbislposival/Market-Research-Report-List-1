<p><p>The medical ventilator market is categorized into non-invasive and invasive ventilators. Non-invasive medical ventilators are designed to support patients without requiring intubation, using interfaces like masks or nasal prongs, making them suitable for those with respiratory issues who still can breathe partially. In contrast, invasive medical ventilators involve the insertion of a tube into the airway, providing more controlled ventilation for critically ill patients or those undergoing surgery, ensuring effective respiratory support in severe cases.</p></p>
