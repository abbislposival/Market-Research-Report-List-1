<p><strong>The Galvanized Reinforcing Bar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li><li>Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized reinforcing bar market consists of two primary types: Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar and Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar. Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar is produced by immersing the rebar in molten zinc, providing a protective coating that enhances corrosion resistance. In contrast, Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar features a continuous process where the rebar is continuously drawn through a bath of molten zinc, ensuring uniform coating and higher efficiency. Both types offer enhanced durability for construction applications.</p></p>
