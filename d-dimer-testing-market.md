<p><p>D-dimer testing is a diagnostic tool used to measure the presence of D-dimer, a fibrin degradation product present in the blood after a blood clot dissolves. The test is invaluable in emergency medicine and critical care for assessing thrombotic disorders.</p><p>The D-dimer testing market is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors such as the rising prevalence of thromboembolic disorders, increased awareness among healthcare professionals, and advancements in diagnostic technologies.
