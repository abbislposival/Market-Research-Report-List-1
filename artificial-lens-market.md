<p><p>The artificial lens market is categorized into several types. Monofocal artificial lenses provide a single focal point, ideal for correcting distance vision. Multifocal artificial lenses offer multiple focal points for near and far vision, catering to presbyopia. Toric artificial lenses correct astigmatism by combining spherical and cylindrical optics. Accommodative artificial lenses change focus with eye movement, mimicking natural vision adjustment. Other lenses encompass specialized designs for unique vision needs, enhancing options for patients undergoing cataract surgery or refractive procedures.</p></p>
