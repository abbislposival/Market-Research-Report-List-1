<p><strong>The Residential Smoke Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Residential Smoke Alarm Market comprises three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric alarms use a light source to detect smoke, responding faster to smoldering fires. Ionization alarms employ radioactive materials to sense fast-flaming fires but may be slower with smoldering types. Combination alarms integrate both technologies for enhanced detection capabilities, providing comprehensive protection against various fire scenarios, making them a popular choice among homeowners for safety and reliability.</p></p>
