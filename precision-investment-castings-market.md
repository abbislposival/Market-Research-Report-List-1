<p><strong>The Precision Investment Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sodium Silicate Process</li><li>Tetraethyl Orthosilicate/ Silica Sol Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision investment castings involve various manufacturing processes, notably the sodium silicate process and the tetraethyl orthosilicate/silica sol process. The sodium silicate method utilizes a water glass solution for creating molds, offering durability and good dimensional accuracy, making it ideal for intricate designs. In contrast, the tetraethyl orthosilicate/silica sol process employs a sol-gel technique, enabling the production of highly precise and smooth surface finishes. Both methods cater to diverse industries, ensuring tailored solutions for complex component production.</p></p>
