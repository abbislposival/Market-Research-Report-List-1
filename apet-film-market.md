<p><strong>The APET Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>APET Sheet</li><li>APET Roll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>APET film market is segmented into two primary types: APET sheet and APET roll. APET sheets are rigid, flat, and often used for applications like packaging, displays, and signage, offering excellent clarity and strength. On the other hand, APET rolls are flexible films commonly utilized in packaging applications requiring thermoforming, such as food containers and blister packs. Both types leverage the properties of APET, including recyclability and chemical resistance, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
