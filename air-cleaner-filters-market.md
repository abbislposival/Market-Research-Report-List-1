<p><p>The air cleaner filters market includes various types designed for different filtration needs. Pre-filters capture larger particles and prolong the life of subsequent filters. Sub-HEPA filters provide enhanced filtration for fine particles not captured by standard filters. Secondary filters act as additional layers in air purification systems. HEPA filters are essential for trapping 99.97% of particles, while ULPA filters offer even finer filtration for ultra-small particles. Other filters may include activated carbon and electrostatic options, catering to specific air quality requirements.</p></p>
