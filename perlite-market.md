<p><p>The perlite market can be segmented into two primary types: crude perlite and expanded perlite. Crude perlite consists of raw volcanic glass, usually harvested from mines, and is characterized by its lightweight and porous nature. In contrast, expanded perlite undergoes a heating process that causes it to expand significantly, resulting in a highly versatile material used in construction, horticulture, and various industrial applications. Both types serve different purposes, catering to distinct customer needs across multiple industries.</p></p>
