<p><p>The Low Speed AEB (Autonomous Emergency Braking) system market includes three key types: Forward Collision Warning (FCW), Dynamic Brake Support (DBS), and Crash Imminent Braking (CIB). FCW alerts drivers of potential collisions using sensors. DBS enhances braking force when the driver applies insufficient pressure. CIB automatically applies brakes to prevent or mitigate collisions if the driver fails to respond. These systems enhance safety by reducing low-speed accidents, improving driver response, and minimizing collision impact in urban and congested traffic scenarios.</p></p>
