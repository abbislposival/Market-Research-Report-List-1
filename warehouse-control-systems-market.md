<p><p>Warehouse Control Systems (WCS) can be categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise WCS solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and integration with existing systems but often requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance efforts. In contrast, Cloud-Based WCS solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, lower initial costs, and easier updates. Each type caters to different operational needs and resource availability.</p></p>
