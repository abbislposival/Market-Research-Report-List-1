<p><p>The DAC market comprises several types, including R-2R and High-Speed Current-Steering architectures. R-2R DACs utilize a resistive ladder network for conversion, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for lower-speed applications. In contrast, High-Speed Current-Steering DACs excel in high-performance scenarios, providing rapid voltage conversion through precise current control, suitable for audio and communication technologies. Additionally, the "Other" segment encompasses various specialized DAC designs, catering to specific needs in industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics markets, enhancing versatility and performance.</p></p>
