<p><strong>The Hypespectral Imaging Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visible/Near Infrared (VIS/NIR)</li><li>Short Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Ultraviolet (UV)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hyperspectral imaging cameras are categorized by the wavelength ranges they capture. Visible/Near Infrared (VIS/NIR) cameras capture light in the visible spectrum and near-infrared, useful for agriculture and environmental monitoring. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) cameras detect light beyond NIR, suitable for material identification and detection of moisture. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) cameras operate in the 3-5 micron range, commonly used in defense and surveillance. Ultraviolet (UV) cameras capture wavelengths below visible light, often used in scientific research and biometrics. Other market types may include specific tailored technologies for niche applications.</p></p>
