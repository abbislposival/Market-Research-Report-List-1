<p><ul><li>Strip-Cut</li><li>Cross-Cut</li><li>Micro-Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper shredder market consists of three main types: strip-cut, cross-cut, and micro-cut shredders. Strip-cut shredders slice paper into long, narrow strips, providing basic security but easily readable by skilled individuals. Cross-cut shredders offer enhanced security by cutting paper into smaller, confetti-like pieces, making reconstruction difficult. Micro-cut shredders provide the highest level of security, rendering documents into tiny particles, ideal for sensitive information. Each type caters to different security needs and user preferences in document disposal.</p></p>
