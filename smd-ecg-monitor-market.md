<p><p>The SMD ECG monitor market encompasses various types of ECG testing devices, primarily focusing on Holter ECG testers and static ECG testers. Holter ECG testers are portable devices that continuously record heart activity over 24 to 48 hours, allowing for dynamic monitoring of arrhythmias and other cardiac conditions. Static ECG testers, on the other hand, provide a snapshot of the heart's electrical activity at a specific moment, typically used in clinical settings for immediate assessment. Both serve crucial roles in cardiac diagnostics.</p></p>
