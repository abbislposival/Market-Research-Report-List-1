<p><ul><li>Parallel shaft</li><li>Bevel woem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The servo gearmotors market is primarily categorized into two types: parallel shaft and bevel gearmotors. Parallel shaft gearmotors feature a direct alignment of the shaft with the motor, providing efficient power transmission and compact design. They are widely used in applications requiring high torque and space efficiency. Bevel gearmotors, on the other hand, utilize angular gears to change the direction of the output shaft, making them ideal for applications with spatial constraints and complex mounting configurations. Both types offer precision control and reliability in motion systems.</p></p>
