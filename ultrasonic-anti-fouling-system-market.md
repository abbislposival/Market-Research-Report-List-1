<p><ul><li>Open Water Transducer</li><li>Contact Transducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Anti-fouling System market features two primary types of transducers: Open Water and Contact Transducers. Open Water Transducers emit ultrasonic waves underwater, creating a protective zone around vessels, effectively preventing biofouling in larger aquatic environments. In contrast, Contact Transducers are designed for direct attachment to surfaces like boat hulls, providing targeted ultrasonic vibrations that disrupt the adhesion of marine organisms. Both technologies aim to enhance vessel performance and reduce maintenance costs by minimizing biofouling without harmful chemicals.</p></p>
