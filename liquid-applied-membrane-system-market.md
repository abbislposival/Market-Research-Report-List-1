<p><p>The Liquid Applied Membrane System market comprises three main types: bituminous, elastomeric, and cementitious. Bituminous membranes, derived from asphalt, offer waterproofing properties ideal for roofs and foundations. Elastomeric membranes, made of synthetic rubber, provide flexibility and durability, making them suitable for dynamic applications and varying temperatures. Cementitious membranes use a cement-based mix to create a solid barrier against moisture, often utilized in areas exposed to water and high humidity. Each type serves distinct needs in construction and infrastructure protection.</p></p>
