<p><ul><li>Ag Paste</li><li>Al Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar paste market primarily consists of two types: Ag paste and Al paste, essential for photovoltaic (PV) cell production. Ag paste, primarily composed of silver, is used for front contact electrodes, providing high conductivity and efficiency in converting sunlight to electricity. Conversely, Al paste is used for back contact layers, serving as a reflector and improving energy absorption. Both types are critical for enhancing the performance and efficiency of solar cells, driving advancements in renewable energy technology.</p></p>
