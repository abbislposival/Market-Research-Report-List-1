<p><p>A Direct Rotary Dryer is a mechanical device designed to efficiently handle the drying of bulk materials by using a rotating drum heated externally or internally. The materials enter the dryer and are exposed to hot gas, promoting moisture evaporation. This technology is widely used in industries such as mining, agriculture, and chemicals due to its effectiveness in processing various materials, including ores, biomass, and food products.</p><p>The Direct Rotary Dryer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period, driven by increased demand for efficient drying solutions across diverse sectors.
