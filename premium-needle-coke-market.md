<p><p>The Premium Needle Coke market is primarily divided into two types: petroleum-based and coal-based. Petroleum-based needle coke is derived from high-quality oil, offering superior properties like low thermal expansion and high electrical conductivity, making it ideal for applications in lithium-ion batteries and electrodes. In contrast, coal-based needle coke is produced from coal tar pitch and is typically less expensive, but it may not meet the same high-performance standards as its petroleum counterparts, affecting its suitability for advanced applications.</p></p>
