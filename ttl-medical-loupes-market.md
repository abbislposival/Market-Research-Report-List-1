<p><p>TTL medical loupes, used in surgical and dental procedures for improved magnification, come primarily in two types: Galilean and Prismatic lenses. Galilean loupes feature a simpler optical system, providing a lightweight design and a wider field of view, ideal for general use. In contrast, Prismatic loupes utilize prisms for enhanced magnification, offering superior depth perception and image clarity, making them suitable for intricate tasks. Both types cater to specific needs, influencing user preference based on comfort and application.</p></p>
