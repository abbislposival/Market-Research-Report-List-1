<p><p>The OLED coating equipment market encompasses two primary types: organic coating and vacuum coating. Organic coating involves applying organic materials that emit light when energized, crucial for the functioning of OLED displays. Vacuum coating, on the other hand, utilizes vacuum techniques to deposit thin layers of materials, ensuring uniformity and precision crucial for OLED performance. Both methods are vital in enhancing the efficiency, durability, and visual quality of OLED products, catering to the growing demand in the display and lighting industries.</p></p>
