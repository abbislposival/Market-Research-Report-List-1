<p><p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) in healthcare encompasses various market types. Statistically Based NLP utilizes machine learning algorithms and large datasets to identify language patterns, improving the understanding of complex medical terminology. Mixed NLP combines both approaches, leveraging the strengths of rule-based methods and statistical models for enhanced text analysis, ultimately improving patient care and clinical workflows.</p></p>
