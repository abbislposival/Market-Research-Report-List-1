<p><p>The CCTV camera market comprises various types, each serving distinct surveillance needs. Dome cameras are discreet and ideal for indoor use, often mounted on ceilings, providing a 360-degree view. Bullet cameras are long and cylindrical, suitable for outdoor settings, offering focused viewing over longer distances. Box cameras are versatile, allowing for interchangeable lenses, making them customizable for specific requirements. PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras can be remotely controlled to pan, tilt, and zoom, enabling dynamic monitoring of larger areas effectively.</p></p>
