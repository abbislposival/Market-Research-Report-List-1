<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bypass Isolation Transfer Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bypass-Isolation</li><li>Delayed Transition Bypass-Isolation</li><li>Closed Transition Bypass-Isolation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bypass Isolation Transfer Switch (BITS) market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Bypass-Isolation**: This allows maintenance of the switch without interrupting power, using a parallel system for uninterruptible service.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Delayed Transition Bypass-Isolation**: This type provides a brief delay during switching to prevent power surges, ensuring smoother transitions.</p><p>3. **Closed Transition Bypass-Isolation**: This enables seamless switchovers with no power interruption by maintaining connection to both power sources until the transition is complete.</p></p>
