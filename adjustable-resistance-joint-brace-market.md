<p><p>The Adjustable Resistance Joint Brace market includes three main types: head splints, limb splints, and torso splints. Head splints provide stabilization and support for cranial injuries, ensuring proper alignment and reducing movement. Limb splints focus on immobilizing arms and legs to aid in recovery from fractures or strains, while torso splints offer support to the back and abdominal areas, promoting healing for post-surgery patients or those with severe injuries. Each type features adjustable resistance to cater to varying levels of rehabilitation needs.</p></p>
