<p><p>The fixed dehumidifier market consists of several types, each serving specific needs. Desiccant dehumidifiers use absorbent materials to draw moisture from the air, ideal for colder environments. Ventilated dehumidifiers actively exchange indoor air with drier outdoor air, helping to manage humidity levels efficiently. Other types may include hybrid models or advanced technologies tailored for specialized applications, enhancing overall humidity control solutions.</p></p>
