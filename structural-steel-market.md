<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Structural Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>I-Beam</li><li>Angle (L-Shape)</li><li>Hollow Structural Section (HSS) Shape</li><li>Z-Shape</li><li>T-Shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural steel market encompasses various shapes, each serving distinct construction needs. I-Beams offer high strength and stability for vertical and horizontal loads. Angle sections (L-Shape) provide versatility in framing and bracing applications. Hollow Structural Sections (HSS) combine aesthetic appeal with structural integrity, ideal for modern designs. Z-Shapes are commonly used for purlins and girts, offering efficient load distribution. T-Shaped sections are often employed in truss systems and as supports, making them essential for diverse construction projects.</p></p>
