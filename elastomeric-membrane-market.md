<p><p>The elastomeric membrane market is primarily categorized into two types: sheet membranes and liquid-applied membranes. Sheet membranes are flexible, pre-fabricated products made from elastomeric materials that are installed as continuous layers for waterproofing and protection. In contrast, liquid-applied membranes are applied as a liquid that cures to form a seamless, flexible barrier. These types are used in various applications, such as roofing, below-grade waterproofing, and facade protection, offering durability and resistance to environmental conditions.</p></p>
