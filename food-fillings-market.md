<p><p>The food fillings market can be categorized into two main types: bakeable and no bakeable. Bakeable fillings are designed to be mixed into recipes that require cooking or baking, such as pastries, pies, and cakes. They often enhance flavor and texture during the baking process. In contrast, no bakeable fillings are ready-to-use mixtures that can be added to products without further cooking, such as cold desserts, sandwich spreads, or toppings, providing convenience and immediate flavor enhancement.</p></p>
