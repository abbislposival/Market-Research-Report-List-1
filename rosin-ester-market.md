<p><p>The Rosin Ester market consists primarily of two types: Glycerol Ester and Pentaerythritol Ester. Pentaerythritol Esters, formulated from pentaerythritol and rosin, offer enhanced thermal stability and hardness, ideal for applications in adhesives and varnishes.
