<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital print film market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based films. Solvent-based films utilize organic solvents for ink absorption, offering vibrant colors and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based films use water as the primary solvent, resulting in lower toxicity and environmental impact, ideal for indoor applications. Both types cater to different needs in signage, packaging, and promotional materials, reflecting a balance between performance, environmental considerations, and application suitability.</p></p>
