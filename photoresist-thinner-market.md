<p><strong>The Photoresist Thinner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photo Resist</li><li>Negative Photo Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist thinner market is primarily divided into two types: positive photoresist and negative photoresist. Positive photoresist becomes soluble when exposed to light, allowing for the creation of intricate patterns on substrates, ideal for applications like semiconductors. Conversely, negative photoresist hardens upon exposure, leading to the removal of the unexposed areas, which is beneficial for producing durable structures. Both types play crucial roles in photolithography processes, catering to diverse industries such as electronics and microfabrication.</p></p>
