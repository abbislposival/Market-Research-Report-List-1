<p><ul><li>Active Amplifier</li><li>Passive Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-frequency power amplifier market consists of two primary types: active amplifiers and passive amplifiers. Active amplifiers utilize external power sources to boost signal strength, providing higher gain and improved performance, making them suitable for applications like telecommunications and broadcasting. Conversely, passive amplifiers do not require external power; they enhance signals through reactive components like inductors and capacitors, offering lower amplification levels and less distortion, often used in applications where minimal signal alteration is desired.</p></p>
