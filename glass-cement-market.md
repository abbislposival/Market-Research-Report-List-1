<p><p>Glass cement, also known as glass-ionomer cement, is a versatile material widely used in construction and dental applications. It is composed of a mixture of glass powder and an organic acid, offering excellent adhesion, durability, and aesthetic properties. The rising focus on eco-friendly construction practices and the need for durable, weather-resistant adhesives are key factors propelling market expansion. Additionally, the growing dental care industry, particularly in emerging economies, is contributing to the demand for glass cement.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of modified glass-ionomer cements with enhanced mechanical properties and faster setting times. Innovations in nanotechnology are also being explored to improve the material's performance. The Asia-Pacific region is emerging as a significant market due to rapid urbanization and infrastructure development, while North America and Europe continue to dominate due to advanced construction and dental care sectors.</p></p>
