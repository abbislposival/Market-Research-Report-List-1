<p><p>The Fertilizer Granulating Aids market is categorized into two primary types: liquid and powder. Liquid granulating aids are often used to enhance the coating process, ensuring better adhesion and uniformity in granule size, leading to improved nutrient delivery. Powder-type aids, on the other hand, serve to bind particles, facilitating the formation of granules during the granulation process. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing fertilizer production, impacting efficiency, effectiveness, and overall agricultural yield.</p></p>
