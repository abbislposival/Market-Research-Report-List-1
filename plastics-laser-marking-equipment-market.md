<p><p>The plastics laser marking equipment market includes two primary types: CO2 and solid-state lasers. CO2 lasers utilize a gas mixture to produce a high-quality beam, ideal for marking non-metal materials like plastics, providing deep engraving and vibrant contrasts. Solid-state lasers, on the other hand, use solid gain mediums, delivering high precision and speed, suitable for various plastic types. Both technologies offer unique advantages, catering to diverse industry needs for permanent, high-resolution markings on plastic surfaces.</p></p>
