<p><p>The coil cleaners market is categorized into three primary types: acid-based cleaners, non-acid cleaners, and self-rinsing cleaners. Acid-based cleaners effectively remove tough deposits like rust and scale by utilizing acidic solutions. Non-acid cleaners, on the other hand, are milder and often suited for sensitive surfaces, providing effective cleaning without corrosion. Self-rinsing cleaners offer the advantage of easy application, requiring no additional rinsing process, making them convenient for quick maintenance without the need for water cleanup.</p></p>
