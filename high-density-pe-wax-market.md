<p><p>High Density PE Wax is categorized into three main types based on production methods. Polymerization High Density PE Wax is synthesized through polymerization of ethylene, offering high molecular weight and stability. Modification High Density PE Wax involves chemical alteration of existing PE wax to enhance specific properties like adhesion or compatibility. Thermal Cracking High Density PE Wax is produced by breaking down high molecular weight polyethylene into lower molecular weight wax, providing excellent lubrication and processing benefits. Each type serves distinct industrial applications.</p></p>
