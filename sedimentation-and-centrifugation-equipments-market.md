<p><p>The sedimentation and centrifugation equipment market includes several key types designed for separating solids from liquids. Clarifiers are used for large-scale sedimentation processes, efficiently settling solids through gravity. Disk centrifuges feature rotating disks for enhanced separation speed. Decanters facilitate continuous solid-liquid separation via gravity and centrifugal force. Dissolved air flotation systems remove suspended solids by creating micro-bubbles that lift them to the surface. Hydrocyclones utilize centrifugal force to segregate particles based on size and density for efficient separation.</p></p>
