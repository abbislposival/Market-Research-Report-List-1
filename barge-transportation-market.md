<p><ul><li>Open Barge</li><li>Covered Barge</li><li>Tank Barge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barge transportation encompasses various market types, each serving distinct needs. Open barges are designed for bulk cargo like coal and gravel, allowing for easy loading and unloading but providing minimal protection from the elements. Covered barges, on the other hand, feature a roof or tarp, making them suitable for goods requiring protection from weather, such as agricultural products or construction materials. Tank barges are specialized for transporting liquids, including chemicals or petroleum, equipped with tanks to ensure safe and efficient storage and transport.</p></p>
