<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Audio Amplifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class-A</li><li>Class-B</li><li>Class-A/B</li><li>Class-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle audio amplifier market comprises several types based on efficiency and performance. Class-A amplifiers offer high sound quality but are energy-inefficient, making them less common in vehicles. Class-B amplifiers improve efficiency by amplifying half of the signal, but can introduce distortion. Class-A/B amplifiers combine both, delivering quality with better efficiency. Class-D amplifiers use pulse width modulation for high efficiency and compact size, making them ideal for modern vehicles, balancing sound quality and power consumption effectively.</p></p>
