<p><ul><li>Cold Cathode Vacuum Gauge</li><li>Hot Cathode Vacuum Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high vacuum gauge market is primarily segmented into two types: cold cathode vacuum gauges and hot cathode vacuum gauges. Cold cathode gauges utilize a discharge process to operate at high vacuum levels, offering high sensitivity and a broad pressure range. They are robust and require minimal maintenance. In contrast, hot cathode gauges rely on a heated filament to ionize gas molecules, providing rapid response and accurate measurements at lower pressures. Each type caters to specific applications in industries like semiconductor manufacturing and research.</p></p>
