<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) equipment market encompasses various deposition techniques, primarily sputtering deposition and evaporation deposition. Sputtering deposition involves ejecting material from a target onto a substrate using energetic ions, creating thin films with excellent adhesion and uniformity. In contrast, evaporation deposition involves the thermal heating of materials until they vaporize and condense onto a substrate, producing films with varying compositions. Both processes are widely used in industries like electronics, optics, and coatings, driving demand for advanced PVD systems.</p></p>
