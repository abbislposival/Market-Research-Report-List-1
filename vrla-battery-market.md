<p><ul><li>Absorbed Glass Mat Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) battery market primarily consists of Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize glass mat separators to absorb electrolyte, providing high discharge rates and durability, making them suitable for applications like UPS systems and solar energy. Gel batteries, containing a silica gel electrolyte, offer a sealed design and excellent deep-cycle capabilities, ideal for renewable energy systems and electric vehicles. Both types are maintenance-free and environmentally friendly, catering to diverse energy storage needs.</p></p>
