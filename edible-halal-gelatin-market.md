<p><p>The edible halal gelatin market is primarily categorized into acid hydrolysis and alkaline hydrolysis types. Acid hydrolysis gelatin is produced by breaking down collagen using acids, resulting in a quicker extraction process and a more refined product. In contrast, alkaline hydrolysis gelatin involves the use of alkaline solutions, often yielding a higher gel strength but requiring a longer processing time. Both types cater to the growing demand for halal-certified products, serving food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries.</p></p>
