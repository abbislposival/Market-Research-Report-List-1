<p><ul><li>FLA batteries</li><li>VRLA batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lead-acid battery market comprises two main types: Flooded Lead-Acid (FLA) batteries and Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid (VRLA) batteries. FLA batteries are traditional, requiring regular maintenance and electrolyte monitoring, making them ideal for high-capacity applications. In contrast, VRLA batteries, which include Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries, are sealed and maintenance-free, providing greater safety and convenience. Both types serve diverse industries, including telecommunications, renewable energy, and material handling, with distinct advantages tailored to specific application needs.</p></p>
