<p><p>The solvent-free resins market is categorized into three main types: single component, two components, and three components. Single component resins are ready to use and require no mixing, offering convenience and quick application. Two component resins consist of a base and hardener that need to be mixed before application, providing enhanced performance and durability. Three component systems typically include a base, hardener, and an additive or modifier, allowing for tailored properties and optimized performance for specific applications.</p></p>
