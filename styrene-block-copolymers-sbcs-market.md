<p><p>Styrene Block Copolymers (SBCs) are versatile materials combining hard and soft segments, offering unique properties such as elasticity and flexibility. The main types include Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), known for its rubber-like qualities; Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS), valued for its high elasticity; Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene (SEBS), which is thermally stable and chemically resistant; and Styrene-Ethylene-Propylene-Styrene (SEPS), featuring enhanced processing capabilities. These SBC variants find applications in adhesives, sealants, automotive parts, and consumer goods, driving market growth across various sectors.</p></p>
