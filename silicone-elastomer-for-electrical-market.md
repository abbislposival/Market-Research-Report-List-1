<p><p>Silicone elastomers are versatile materials used in the electrical market, categorized primarily into three types: High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV), Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV), and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HTV is known for its high thermal stability and durability, making it ideal for demanding environments. RTV offers easy application and quick curing at ambient temperatures, suitable for general-purpose sealing and bonding. LSR is highly flexible and suitable for intricate designs, providing excellent insulation and mechanical properties in versatile applications.</p></p>
