<p><p>Box and carton overwrap films are essential packaging materials used to protect products during storage and transport. Shrink films are heat-activated, conforming tightly around items when heated, providing a secure seal. They offer tamper evidence and promote product visibility. Stretch films, on the other hand, are elastic polymers that are stretched around items, maintaining tension to secure loads. These films are widely used in pallet wrapping and bundling, offering durability and protection against dust and moisture while allowing easy access to products.</p></p>
