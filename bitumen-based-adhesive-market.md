<p><p>The bitumen-based adhesive market is categorized into three main types: liquid state, paste, and solid state. Liquid state adhesives are easily applied and ideal for thin layers, providing excellent adhesion for various substrates. Paste adhesives offer a thicker consistency, allowing for better control during application and are suitable for vertical surfaces. Solid state adhesives, typically in a solid form, are used in high-performance applications where strong bonding and durability are essential, catering to specialized industries like construction and automotive.</p></p>
