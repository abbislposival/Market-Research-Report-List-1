<p><p>The Full Bridge Driver Market is divided into two primary types: with Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) and without PWM. Full Bridge Drivers with PWM utilize modulation techniques to control the output power and speed of motors efficiently, making them suitable for applications requiring precise control. In contrast, Full Bridge Drivers without PWM operate with fixed voltage and current levels, providing simpler control for applications where precise modulation is unnecessary. Each type serves distinct needs based on application requirements and performance characteristics.</p></p>
